The Calculators

I've made a series of distortion calculators. The forst one is for a single transistor stage, the next ones will be for differential stages and for multiple stages. Over some time, I'll also upload calculators for other types of "distortion", like frequency response, noise, time-resposne and so on.  They will all be based on the types of amplifiers presented on these pages, and the theory that accompanies them.  The calculators will be made as ActiveX controls.  Please inform me if you have problems getting them to work.
