Welcome to the Terje Sandstrøm Audio Technology Web site!

For several years I worked on the design of audio equipment for very high fidelity use, primarily for Electrocompaniet.   As I no longer work actively in that field, I decided to put as much of what I know onto this site, and let everyone have a look.  This site contains information on the audio technology I represent, on  the history of the early Electrocompaniet, and their world famous 25W "Otala" amplifier, which I worked on.  The history of Electrocompaniet   is endorsed by Per Abrahamsen of Electrocompaniet, Mr. EC himself.  He has also provided more material and supplemented what I had.  We decided to release all older schematics and other stuff related to these fantastic amplifiers.  We also decided to release  "unknown" EC designs, that is, designs that for one reason or another never made it into production, but which have the excellence and spirit  of EC.  The schematics are accompanied by theory and anecdotes relating to the designs.

I have also added schematics and other stuff from the period after I left EC, and also some freelancing  stuff.

NEWS (april 2004) :  Correct schematics of the modified amplifiers now on site, including photoes !!!

The schematics of the modified amplifiers were done at the NRF.

I have also made a section on troubleshooting your Electrocompaniet 25W amp. Parts of the info is from the original service manual.

My thesis on the University was also related to audio technology, and I also presented some papers on amplifier technology through the AES.  Stuff from all this is also provided.

Audio and electronic amplifier theory are provided.  Take a look at what is new on this site.
